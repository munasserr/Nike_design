Nike Enters 10-Year Partnership With
Major League Baseball

Beginning in 2020, Nike will become the the exclusive provider
of Major League Baseball's on-field uniforms (which will feature
a Nike Swoosh), including baselayers, game-day outerwear and
training apparel.

“Nike's global brand and reputation as a leader in driving
innovation makes them an ideal partner,” says Baseball
Commissioner Robert D. Manfred, Jr.

Nike will also continue as an official MLB sponsor, supporting
league initiatives, grassroots marketing and fan events. Nike, as
part of the new agreement, will partner with all 30 MLB Clubs
and promote its brand and products across MLB media assets,
including MLB Network, MLB.com and MLB Social.
